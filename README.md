Worked on an end-to-end Excel Business Intelligence dashboard using an Amazon sales dataset.

Focused on cleaning raw data, building PivotTables, calculating key KPIs like total revenue, order count, average order value, and identifying top category and city contributions.

The key learning was understanding how revenue is concentrated across categories and locations, and how KPIs help convert raw numbers into decision-ready insights.

It helped me strengthen my Business Intelligence thinking — not just building charts, but explaining what the data means for decisions.
